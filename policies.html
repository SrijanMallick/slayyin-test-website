Return Policy

Last updated: March 2024

1. Return Eligibility

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for the following reasons:

  • Product received is damaged or defective
  • Wrong item received
  • Size or fit issues (for non-customized items)

2. Return Process

To initiate a return:

  • Contact our customer service within 7 days of delivery
  • Provide order number and reason for return
  • Follow the return instructions provided

3. Refund Policy

Refunds will be processed:

  • Within 7-10 business days after receiving the returned item
  • To the original payment method
  • Minus any shipping charges (unless the return is due to our error)

4. Exceptions

The following items are not eligible for returns:

  • Customized or personalized items
  • Items marked as final sale
  • Items that have been used or damaged after delivery
