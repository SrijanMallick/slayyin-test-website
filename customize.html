Frequently Asked Questions

What's the minimum order quantity for custom merchandise?

Our minimum order quantity is typically 50 pieces per design for most products like t-shirts and hoodies. For premium items, we may have different minimums. For specific requirements, please contact us.

How long does it take to produce custom college merchandise?

Production time varies based on quantity and complexity, but typically takes 2-3 weeks after design approval. For urgent orders, we offer express services at an additional cost.

Do you help with design or should we provide our own artwork?

We offer comprehensive design services! Our team can create custom artwork based on your ideas, or you can provide your own designs. If you have a college logo or specific design requirements, we'll work with you to bring your vision to life.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, UPI, and major credit/debit cards. For institutional orders, we can accommodate purchase orders and provide GST invoices as required.

Can we see samples before placing a large order?

Yes! We offer digital proofs for design approval at no cost. For physical samples, there's a nominal fee that can be credited toward your final order. Sample production typically takes 5-7 business days.
